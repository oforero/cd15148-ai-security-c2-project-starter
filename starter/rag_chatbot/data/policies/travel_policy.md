# FinanceGuard Inc. — Corporate Travel Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Policy Owner:** Finance Department
**Applies To:** All employees traveling on company business

## Booking Requirements

All business travel must be booked at least 14 calendar days in advance through the company's designated travel portal (Concur). Last-minute bookings (under 7 days) require director-level approval and a written justification.

## Airfare

- Economy class is the standard for all domestic flights and international flights under 6 hours.
- Premium economy is permitted for international flights exceeding 6 hours.
- Business class requires VP approval and is limited to C-suite executives and flights over 10 hours.
- Employees should book the lowest logical airfare. Choosing a more expensive flight for schedule convenience is acceptable if the difference is under $150.

## Lodging

- Hotel rates must not exceed $250 per night in standard markets and $350 per night in high-cost cities (New York, San Francisco, London, Tokyo).
- Extended stays (5+ nights) should use negotiated corporate rates or apartment-style accommodations.
- Room service and minibar charges are not reimbursable.

## Ground Transportation

- Rental cars are approved only when public transit or rideshare is impractical (e.g., rural locations, multiple site visits). Mid-size vehicles or smaller; SUVs require justification.
- Fuel for rental cars is reimbursable. Premium fuel is not unless required by the rental agreement.
- Rideshare (Uber, Lyft) is preferred for airport transfers and local travel.

## Per Diem Rates

For meals during travel, employees may claim the GSA per diem rate for the destination city rather than submitting individual receipts. Current domestic per diem ranges from $59 to $79 per day depending on location. International per diem follows the State Department DSSR rates.

## Submission Deadline

All travel expense reports must be submitted within 30 calendar days of trip completion. Reports submitted after 30 days may be denied. Reports over 60 days will not be processed under any circumstances.
