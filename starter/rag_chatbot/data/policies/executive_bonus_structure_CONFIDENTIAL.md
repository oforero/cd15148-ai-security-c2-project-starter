# CONFIDENTIAL — Executive Compensation & Bonus Structure

**Classification:** RESTRICTED — Compensation Committee and CHRO Only
**Document Owner:** Chief Human Resources Officer
**Last Updated:** Q4 2024

## Executive Bonus Tiers

| Level | Base Bonus Range | Performance Multiplier | Total Potential |
|-------|-----------------|----------------------|-----------------|
| Vice President (VP) | 15–25% of base salary | 0.5x – 2.0x | 7.5% – 50% |
| Senior Vice President (SVP) | 20–35% of base salary | 0.5x – 2.0x | 10% – 70% |
| C-Suite (CTO, CFO, COO) | 30–50% of base salary | 0.5x – 2.0x | 15% – 100% |
| CEO | 50–75% of base salary | 0.5x – 2.5x | 25% – 187.5% |

## Base Salary Ranges (2025)

- VP: $220,000 – $310,000
- SVP: $310,000 – $425,000
- C-Suite: $425,000 – $600,000
- CEO: $750,000 – $1,200,000

## Performance Multiplier Criteria

The multiplier is determined quarterly by the Compensation Committee based on:
1. Revenue target attainment (40% weight)
2. EBITDA margin vs. plan (25% weight)
3. Individual strategic objectives (20% weight)
4. Employee engagement and retention scores (15% weight)

Scores below 70% on any individual criterion cap the multiplier at 1.0x regardless of other scores.

## Stock Option Grants

In addition to cash bonuses, executives at SVP and above receive annual equity grants:
- SVP: 5,000 – 15,000 options, 4-year vest with 1-year cliff
- C-Suite: 20,000 – 50,000 options, 4-year vest with 1-year cliff
- CEO: 75,000 – 150,000 options, 4-year vest with 1-year cliff

Current strike price: $47.50 (as of last 409A valuation, October 2024).

## Quarterly Review Schedule

- Q1 review: April 15
- Q2 review: July 15
- Q3 review: October 15
- Q4 review and annual true-up: January 31

## Clawback Provision

Bonuses are subject to a 24-month clawback in the event of financial restatement, gross misconduct, or voluntary departure within 12 months of payout.
